About Me

I am a frontend developer based in Awka, Anambra state. I am skilled in the frontend programming languages, HTML, CSS, JS and their various frameworks. I am also a master designer who creates high quality and pro user interface designs.

Since the beginning of my programming journey, I've done amazing works for entepreneurs and budding businesses making them shine brightly in the digital realm with my clean design patterns, simple content structures and thoughtful user friendly interactions.
Making websites is my hobby, I love bringing cool ideas to life on a browser.
